Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about payouts, eligibility, program details, and manual download setups.

To register, visit the Excellence Financing Broker partner page (available via app.efb.ae/auth/become-partner) and complete the online registration form in approximately 1 minute.

You will need to verify your email, supply accurate contact details, and input UAE residential address and bank credentials for payout distributions.

The EFB BDP Program is open to everyone residing in the UAE. Requirement criteria are basic:

  • Valid Emirates ID (Residency identification)
  • Verified UAE Mobile Number & Email Address
  • Personal UAE Bank Account to receive payouts

Payouts follow a lucrative standard payout structure based on finalized disbursements:

  • Personal Loan: 0.80% of disbursed loan value
  • Mortgage Loan: 0.35% of disbursed loan value
  • Business Loan: 0.50% of disbursed loan value
  • Credit Card: AED 400 per approved credit card
  • Business Account: AED 500 per approved account activation

Active partners are structured under badge levels (Silver, Gold, Diamond) adding up to an extra 10% bonus on top of payouts.

Standard disbursements are tracked inside your CRM workspace and validated dynamically upon successful completion. Monthly campaigns and volume-slab bonuses are accumulated and paid directly on every 5th of the succeeding month.
Yes! EFB offers a multi-tier referral multiplier. When your referred partners join, they become your Sub-BDP. You earn 5% of your Sub-BDP's payouts on every successful disbursement they perform. Additionally, you earn monthly bonus payouts of up to AED 100 per active Sub-BDP.
