To qualify for a personal loan in the United Arab Emirates, banking authorities and institutions typically require:

  • Minimum Monthly Salary: AED 3,000 – AED 10,000 (differs by bank and listing status of the employer).
  • Legal Status: Must possess a valid UAE residence visa and Emirates ID.
  • Documentation: A Salary Transfer Letter is standard, though non-transfer products exist.
